Our App

SAMVAAD@BGI

Our College Anonymous Messaging App is more than just a communication tool—it's a safe space where students and faculty can share thoughts, ideas, and feedback without hesitation. Whether it’s a suggestion for improving campus life, a question about a class, or simply a kind word to brighten someone’s day, our app makes sure every voice is heard, valued, and respected. With features like anonymous messaging, organized feedback management, and real-time updates, we aim to enhance collaboration, understanding, and well-being across campus—all in a sleek, easy-to-use desktop interface.

About Our College

Buddha Institute of Technology (BIT), approved by A.I.C.T.E. and affiliated with AKTU, is a premier institution recognized nationally and internationally. BIT combines academic excellence with strong industry connections across sectors like Automobile, Aerospace, Defense, Textile, Software, and Manufacturing, providing students with real-world exposure. Life at BIT blends a vibrant campus culture with rigorous academics, fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and innovative learning environment that promotes both personal growth and student well-being.
